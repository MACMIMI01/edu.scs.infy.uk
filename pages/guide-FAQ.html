Frequently Asked Questions

How do I initiate an assessment?
Navigate to the Test Library (for practice) or Exam Library (for formal finals). Select your registered module to begin the secure assessment interface.
Where are my digital result slips located?
Instant performance metrics are displayed upon submission. Official PDF result slips are archived and accessible via your personal Dashboard under the "Results" section.
Can I modify inputs mid-examination?
The system allows real-time modifications. You may use the Navigation Matrix (number panel) to revisit any question before final encryption and submission.
Technical error occurred. What is the protocol?
The SCS CBT system features Auto-Save Progress. If a connection is lost, simply refresh or log back in; your previous inputs remain cached on the central server.

System Deployment Guide

1

Authentication

Establish a secure session using your institutional credentials. Multi-role detection will automatically configure your unique workspace.

2

Module Selection

Browse the synchronized library to find your active assessment. Use the search filters to isolate specific academic sessions or terms.

3

Execution

Proceed through the digitized questions. The system monitors activity and timing parameters to ensure academic integrity.

4

Data Submission

Upon completion, initiate the submission sequence. Results are calculated using our proprietary grading algorithm and stored in the central database.
