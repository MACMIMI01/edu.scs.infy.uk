The Admission Procedure

Our enrollment procedure is open to forward-thinking candidates who seek high standard educational pathways. Complete our process using three key guidelines below:

1

Purchase & Fill Admission Documents

Acquire application guides from our main accounts counter at St. Christopher School Campus, Agbor-Obi, or print guidelines via our digital student portal.

2

Student Assessment & Screening

All child applications undergo basic educational assessment reviews in Mathematics and English Language to gauge standard academic alignment levels.

3

Approval & Document Upload

Upon evaluation success, final administrative offer letters are drafted, after which parents complete onboarding fee terms.
